# ✨ Glow — Your Beauty Guide

A warm, trendy beauty search engine for women aged 25-35 who feel lost in the ocean of beauty information.

**Ask a beauty question → Get a clear, reassuring answer with product & procedure recommendations.**

## Features

- 🔍 Smart search across common beauty concerns
- 💡 Curated expert recommendations (skincare + procedures)
- 🔗 Direct links to products and treatments
- 💝 Sweet, reassuring advice with every answer
- 📱 Fully responsive & mobile-friendly
- 🎨 Trendy, modern design with soft aesthetics

## Topics Covered

- Dark circles & under-eye care
- Acne scars & skin texture
- Anti-aging (late 20s / early 30s)
- Glowing & radiant skin
- Botox & preventive injectables
- Lip fillers
- Pore minimization
- Hyperpigmentation & dark spots
- Dry & dehydrated skin
- Adult acne
- Rosacea & redness
- Hair removal
- And more...

## Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no build step. Just open `index.html` and go.

## Preview

Open `index.html` in your browser, or use the GitHub Pages deployment.

## Disclaimer

Glow is for informational purposes only. Always consult a dermatologist for personalized medical advice.
